- **Profile**: TBD (self-improvement/autonomy)

## Boundary

L2 defines self-improvement boundaries and safety constraints. L1 implements
Symbiant integration under strict FIC governance.

## Integration Points

| Point | L2 Specifies | L1 Implements |
|-------|--------------|---------------|
| Improvement scope | What can be improved | Execution mechanics |
| Safety constraints | What must not change | Enforcement |
| Evaluation criteria | Success measurement | Reporting |

## Constraints

1. L2 must not enable autonomous self-modification.
2. All Symbiant integration requires an L1 FIC with critical risk review.
3. L0 is off-limits to Symbiant.
